\texttt{memory\_score} & numeric & Composite episodic memory score &
\textasciitilde50--120 & Outcome variable; higher = better memory \\
\texttt{hippocampal\_volume} & numeric & Hippocampal volume from MRI &
\textasciitilde6--9 mm³ & Predictor of interest; affected by age and
ICV \\
\texttt{age\_group} & categorical & Age stratum & \texttt{"Young"}
(\textless50), \texttt{"Older"} (≥50) & Key confounder in analysis \\
\texttt{sex} & binary / factor & Biological sex & 0 = female, 1 = male &
May have small/no effect on memory \\
\texttt{education\_years} & numeric & Years of formal education &
\textasciitilde10--20 & Small positive effect on memory \\
\texttt{ICV} & numeric & Intracranial volume & \textasciitilde1300--1800
mm³ & Covariate; larger in males; influences hippocampal volume \\
\texttt{motion} & numeric & Head motion during MRI &
\textasciitilde0--0.4 & Minor noise; fMRI nuisance variable \\
\texttt{sleep\_hours} & numeric & Average sleep per night &
\textasciitilde5--9 hours & Irrelevant noise variable \\
\texttt{stress\_score} & numeric & Self-reported stress &
\textasciitilde30--70 & Irrelevant noise variable \\
\texttt{handedness} & binary / factor & Dominant hand & 0 = right, 1 =
left & Irrelevant noise variable \\
